Tom Brennan 5k New Year's Day Race Results

Congratulations to the individual winners of our annual Tom Brennan 5km race today in the Phoenix Park. Abdel Laadjel of Donore Harriers was the winner of the men's race in a time of 14.54, with Edel Monaghan of Celtic DCH AC was the winner of our women's race in a time of 17.06.

We were delighted to have Tom's son John, his wife Marie and the children Caoimhe, Michael and Thomas with us to make the presentations.

Results are available on MyRunResults here

Thank you all for your support and we are looking forward to seeing you at many races throughout 2023 and back in the Phoenix Park on New Year's Day 2024 when Liffey Valley AC will start into our 50th year.

Happy New Year from all at Liffey Valey AC
