Now for the Track!

The Irish Summer is finally here and we can look forward to lots of races on the track.LVAC 2022 Cross Country Championskicked off on April 27th and it was great to see some PB's posted on the back of of work put into the cross country season.

Looking back through April, there was a lot to cheer.

well done to our athletes who competed in the Paris Marathon on April 3rd.

There was M50 Team Gold in the AAI National 10k incorporated into the Great Ireland Run with Tom winning gold and Arnaud taking silver in their individual categories. Joe Cooper is the LVAC Winter League Champion 2022 having won the last 2 Mile Road Race.

Congratulations to Lea Fulcher who won gold in the M45 race and also scored on the Dublin Team who claimed silver at the Leinster Novice Masters Road Championships in Gowran, Co. Kilkenny on April 30th
