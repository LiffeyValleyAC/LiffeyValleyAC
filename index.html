Hello Spring, Hello Road

The opening 2 months of 2022 have been great so far, with top performances and medals to show from the various cross country championship races. Heading into March/April, the focus switches to the road with brighter evenings ahead.

April will be a busy month of racing with the AAI National 10k incorporated into the Great Ireland Run taking place on April 9th, while our club races continue within the LVAC Winter League 2022

On April 18th the Leinster 10 Mile Road Race incorporated into the Battle of Clontarf 10 miler takes place.

On April 24th the AAI Road Relays are due to take place, while on April 30th the Leinster Novice Masters Road Championships take place in Gowran, Co. Kilkenny.
