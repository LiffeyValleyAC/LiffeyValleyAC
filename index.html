Welcome to 2022

Thank you to everyone who took part in another successful staging of our annual New Years Day Race which remembers our legendary athlete Tom Brennan

Special thanks also to the Lord Mayor of Dublin, Alison Gilliland and her Team who have selected our race as their first event of their now annual Lord Mayors 5 Alive challenge

Having taken individual golds and silver plus team bronze at the Dublin Masters XC we enjoyed further success at the Leinster Intermediate and Masters XC

Next up is the 38th Raheny 5 Mile, while some Master Athletes may target the National Masters Indoor Championships on the same day at Athlone International Arena
