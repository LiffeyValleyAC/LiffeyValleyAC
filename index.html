Liffey Valley Athletics Club are an Islandbridge based club welcoming new members.

We welcome runners of all ages who are looking to improve their running through accessible, structured training sessions within a welcoming and encouraging environment.

We compete year-round in cross-country, track and road races. We have expert coaches who plan training to help you race to your full potential

If you would like to experience the unique training atmosphere you will find in our club, why not come along to our Tuesday and Thursday Training Sessions

We meet at 6.45pm at our club house in Islandbridge

We are located near the Phoenix Park or meet us on Facebook
