Tom Brennan Memorial Race - FAQ

Q Are race entries accepted on the morning of the race?
A No race entries are taken on race day as race entries must be registered via the Event Master platform. Entries close on December 30th or earlier if the race sells before this date.
Q Where is the race taking place?
A Furze Road, which is just off Chestrfield Avenue in the Phoenix Park
Q What time does the race start?
A 9.30AM
