The Course

FAQ

Q Are race entries accepted on the morning of the race?
A Unfortunately, this is not possible. Race entries must be registered via the Event Master platform. Entries close on December 30th or earlier, if the race sells out before this date.
Q Where is the race taking place?
A The race will start on the Furze Road, which is just off Chesterfield Avenue in the Phoenix Park.
Q What time does the race start?
A The race starts at 09.30 AM
Q If I missed a postage entry, where do I collect my number?
A The Number Collection area is on the Furze Road/Chesterfield Avenue Junction. Please have your number (sent to you by SMS Text). Please arrive early to prevent delays and queues.
Q What time can I collect my number?
A Number Collection will be open from 8.00 AM
Q Where is the start line?
A The start line is 500 meters from the Number Collection area on the Furze Road/Chesterfield Avenue Junction.
Q Where can I park my car?
A Please visit Phoenix Park Opening Hours for details on car parking. NOTE: Knockmaroon Car Park will be closed.
Q Where do I collect my race gift?
A Please collect your long sleeve t-shirt AFTER you have crossed the finish line and completed the race.
Q Will there be water?
A Yes, water will be available approximately 20 meters after the finish line.
Q Will there be toilets?
A Yes, there will be portaloos available on Furze Road. There are also toilets at the Papal Cross Car Park.
